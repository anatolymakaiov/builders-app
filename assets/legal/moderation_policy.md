# Moderation Policy

Template document — legal review required before public launch.

## Scope

Moderation may apply to job posts, profiles, applications, offers, chats, media uploads, support requests, complaints, reports, admin inbox messages, billing requests, and account behaviour.

## Job Moderation

Admin review may approve, reject, hold, pause, unpublish, or remove jobs. Rejected or held jobs may include an admin explanation sent through notifications or inbox messages.

## User Moderation

Admins may restrict, suspend, delete, or anonymise accounts where needed for safety, legal compliance, fraud prevention, non-payment, policy breaches, complaints, or platform security.

## Records

Moderation decisions and related evidence may be retained for audit, safety, legal, support, and dispute purposes.
