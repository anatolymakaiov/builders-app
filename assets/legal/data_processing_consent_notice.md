# Data Processing & Consent Notice

Template document — legal review required before public launch.

## Overview

Stroyka UK Ltd processes data to operate the STROYKA construction jobs marketplace. Some processing is necessary to provide the app. Some optional features may rely on consent or user choices.

## Processing Activities

The app may process profile data, company data, job posts, applications, offers, teams, chats, media uploads, support requests, complaints, reports, billing/payment metadata, location-related data, notification tokens, and legal acceptance records.

## Consent Areas

Consent or user choices may be relevant for push notifications, optional media uploads, optional location features, marketing or policy notifications where applicable, and future non-essential cookies or analytics. Consent can be withdrawn where the law allows, but some app features may stop working.

## Essential Processing

Authentication, profile creation, job posting, applications, offers, moderation, support, billing records, legal acceptance records, fraud prevention, and security may be processed because they are necessary for the service, legal compliance, or legitimate interests.

## Service Providers

Firebase, Firebase Storage, Firebase Cloud Messaging, Google Cloud, payment processors, and other service providers may process data on behalf of Stroyka UK Ltd.
