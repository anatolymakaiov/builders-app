# Company Information

This is a draft document for a UK-based construction jobs marketplace app. It must be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before production launch.

## App Name

STROYKA.

## Business Purpose

STROYKA is designed as a UK construction jobs marketplace connecting workers, teams, employers, and company users through profiles, job posts, applications, offers, chat, alerts, support requests, moderation, and billing features.

## Company Details

Final legal company name, registered company number, registered office address, trading address, VAT number if applicable, contact email, support email, and telephone number must be added before production launch.

## Technology Providers

The app uses Firebase, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Authentication, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and may use Stripe or another future payment provider.

## Support and Complaints

Users may contact support through the app. Complaint handling and admin review features are available or planned for platform safety and compliance.

## Legal Documents

The app includes draft legal sections for privacy, terms, conduct, worker terms, employer posting policy, refunds, complaints, cookies, data retention, and UK privacy notice. These must be legally reviewed before launch.
