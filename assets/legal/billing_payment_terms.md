# Billing & Payment Terms

Template document — legal review required before public launch.

## Scope

These terms apply to employer plans, publication packages, job slots, manual invoices, direct debit requests, billing support, payment reminders, and payment metadata.

## Payment Provider

Payments may be handled by Stripe or another future payment provider. STROYKA must not store full card details. Payment provider terms may also apply.

## Plans and Job Slots

Plan limits, job slots, publication rules, trial terms, payment status, and moderation status may affect whether a job is visible to workers. Pending moderation jobs may not count as active public jobs unless final business rules say otherwise.

## Invoices and Direct Debit

Employers are responsible for keeping billing information accurate and paying invoices or direct debits when due. Billing requests may be reviewed by admins.

## Non-payment

Non-payment may result in plan limits, job visibility restrictions, paused publication, account restriction, or debt recovery steps, subject to final legal review.
