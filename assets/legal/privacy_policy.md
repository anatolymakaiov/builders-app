# Privacy Policy

This is a draft document for a UK-based construction jobs marketplace app. It must be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before production launch.

## Who We Are

STROYKA is a construction jobs marketplace that helps workers, teams, employers, and company users connect around UK construction job opportunities. The app supports worker profiles, employer profiles, job posts, applications, chats, media uploads, moderation, support requests, complaints, alerts, and billing features.

## What Personal Data We Collect

We may collect account details such as name, email address, phone number, role, profile photo, trade, qualifications, certifications, work history, education, location, company details, billing status, support requests, reports, and app activity.

Workers may add portfolio images, team information, references, experience, and application data. Employers may add company profile data, job posts, site addresses, gallery images, billing plan information, and application decisions.

## Firebase and Storage

The app uses Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Cloud Messaging, and related Firebase tools. Firebase Storage may store uploaded files such as profile photos, company photos, job photos, chat media, worker portfolio photos, team portfolio photos, videos, and voice messages.

## How We Use Data

We use data to operate accounts, show job posts, process applications, support negotiations, display profiles, enable chat, send alerts, moderate content, process complaints, manage support requests, and prepare billing or payment requests through Stripe or a future payment provider.

## Legal Bases

We aim to follow UK GDPR principles, including lawfulness, fairness, transparency, purpose limitation, data minimisation, accuracy, storage limitation, integrity, confidentiality, and accountability. Depending on the feature, processing may be based on contract, legitimate interests, consent, or legal obligation.

## Chat and Media

Messages, links, images, videos, and audio messages may be stored and associated with chats, job posts, applications, workers, teams, or employers. Users must not upload illegal, harmful, discriminatory, or unsafe content.

## Moderation, Complaints, and Support

Admins may review job posts, reports, complaints, support requests, payment requests, and app activity where needed to operate the platform, protect users, enforce policies, and comply with legal requirements.

## Payments and Billing

Employer billing and payment request data may include selected plan, payment mode, plan status, available job posts, used job posts, active period, and payment request status. Real payments may be processed by Stripe or another future payment provider. Payment providers may process data under their own privacy terms.

## Sharing Data

We may share relevant profile and job data between workers and employers to enable applications and hiring conversations. We may share data with Firebase, payment providers, analytics or hosting providers, legal advisers, and authorities where required by law.

## Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for account operation, legal compliance, dispute handling, fraud prevention, billing records, moderation, and safety. Some deleted content may remain in backups for a limited period.

## Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion. We may retain limited records where required for legal, billing, fraud prevention, dispute, safety, or compliance reasons.

## Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, object to processing, request portability, and complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office.
