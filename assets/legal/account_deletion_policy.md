# Account Deletion Notice

Template document — legal review required before public launch.

## Permanent Action

Deleting an account is intended to be permanent. A deleted profile cannot be restored by the user.

## What May Be Deleted

Where legally and technically possible, profile data, public profile visibility, photos, portfolio data, company profile data, and account settings may be deleted or anonymised.

## What May Be Retained

Some records may be retained where needed for legal compliance, billing, accounting, tax, moderation, safety, fraud prevention, complaints, disputes, support history, audit logs, backups, or protection of other users.

## Firebase Authentication

Full Firebase Auth deletion may require recent login. If deletion cannot be completed immediately, the account may be marked as deleted and hidden or anonymised while further steps are completed.
