# Employer Terms

Template document — legal review required before public launch.

## Employer Accounts

Employers and company users may create company profiles, add contact details, gallery images, job posts, site details, billing requests, and application decisions. Employers must keep information accurate and must have authority to represent the company.

## Applications and Offers

Employers may receive worker or team applications, send messages, negotiate, reject, reopen, withdraw offers where supported, and create offers with start date, site address, contact information, and job details.

## Legal Responsibility

Employers remain responsible for job legality, pay, worker checks, right-to-work checks, health and safety, insurance, tax, CIS, employment status, contracts, site rules, and payment obligations.

## No Guarantee

STROYKA does not guarantee that workers will apply, accept offers, attend site, have required qualifications, or be suitable for work. Employers must carry out their own checks.
