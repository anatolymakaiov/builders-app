# Safety & Abuse Reporting Policy

Template document — legal review required before public launch.

## Reporting

Users should report unsafe jobs, abuse, harassment, discrimination, threats, fraud, fake profiles, fake job posts, harmful content, or suspicious behaviour through support or reporting tools.

## Review

Admins may review reports, related profiles, job posts, applications, chats, media uploads, attachments, and support history. Admins may contact users through admin inbox messages.

## Actions

Possible actions include warning, requesting more information, removing content, rejecting job posts, holding job posts, restricting accounts, suspending accounts, deleting accounts, or referring serious issues to authorities.

## Emergency

The app is not an emergency service. If anyone is in immediate danger, users should contact emergency services or the relevant site authority.
