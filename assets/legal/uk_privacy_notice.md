# UK Privacy Notice

This is a draft document for a UK-based construction jobs marketplace app. It must be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before production launch.

## UK GDPR

The app aims to process personal data in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Final controller details, contact details, and any Data Protection Officer details must be added before launch.

## Categories of Data

We may process identity data, contact data, profile data, company data, job post data, application data, chat and media data, billing data, support request data, complaint/report data, moderation data, and technical data.

## Purposes

We process data to create accounts, verify role flows, show jobs, manage applications, enable chat, store profile and portfolio data, moderate job posts, send alerts, handle complaints, manage support requests, and support billing/payment requests.

## Lawful Bases

Possible lawful bases include contract, legitimate interests, consent, legal obligation, and vital interests where applicable. Final lawful bases should be reviewed by legal counsel.

## International Transfers

Firebase, Firebase Storage, Stripe, or future providers may process data outside the UK. Appropriate safeguards should be reviewed before production launch.

## User Rights

Users may have rights to access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, and complaint to the Information Commissioner's Office.

## Security

The app should use reasonable technical and organisational measures, including authentication, Firestore rules, Storage rules, access controls, and moderation tools.

## Contact

Final contact details must be added before production launch.
